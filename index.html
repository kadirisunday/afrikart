Welcome to Afrikarts

Afrikarts work to discover, connect, and mentor the underdeveloped African Talents with digital opportunities and ensure the protection of their rights. We are making the next generation of world-class African Talents. We achieve this through our impactful programs: “Afrik- coach a -winner”,life skills outreaches, talent hunt hour, and other onboarding programs.

WHAT WE DO

DISCOVERING TALENTS

We’re committed to discovery. we’re currently registering upcoming artistes, creators, and developers who have displayed outstanding performance in their work. We give them the opportunity to sell their content to the world.

ADVOCACY & OUTREACH

We give voice to your lyrics and color your art. Through our outreach and consistent advocacy , we make sure we showcase your content to the world . we are an advocate of both digital opportunities and digital rights.

MENTORSHIP & SPONSORSHIP

It’s no secret how far your talent can take you with the right mentorship and sponsorship. We work tirelessly to bring you winning opportunities. We will not just connect you with the right mentor , we will also provide you with the necessary support.

Lend the Helping Hand. Get Involved.
